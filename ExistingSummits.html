Summit 2003 - Late Tahoe



The North American Summit 2003 was based in Lake Tahoe, CA. The first day conference was held at a remote cabin on the western (California) shore. The post conference Festivities were held Caesar's Palace located on the South Shore (Nevada).

Summit 2004 - Vancouver



The North American Summit 2004 was based in Vancouver, BC. We stayed at a posh resort called the Canadian Princess. Each person was randomly paired with another delegate to share the lavish suite style rooms that were reserved for the peace talks. After the yearly round table, the Summit members moved out to sea to test the annual salmon run.

Summit 2005 - Waikiki



The North American Summit 2005 was based in Waikiki, HI. The paradise destination was selected cause the prior Summit meetings had been held in cooler weather for the past three years. The first night began as so many have in the past. A nice dinner, in this case Duke's Barefoot Bar, followed by a long night at Moose McGillicuddys. For those who were there know that this place was the location for AP 2005. This monumental proposition would forever change the 2005 Summit Peace Talks. The following day we set sail aboard the Maggie Joe for some Sport Fishing. This proved to be bad idea after a 12 hour drinking bender. More to come....

Summit 2006 - Calgary

The North American Summit 2006 was based in Calgary, AB. This Summit doubled as a Bachelor Party for the Mexican Captain Wu. The famous Calgary Stampede was in town and what a perfect environment for the Summit Peace Talks. Although the delegates from Canada warned the American delegates of what to expect, there was no way to plan for this momentous summit.

Summit 2007 - Chicago


The North American Summit 2007 was based in Chicago, Illinois. This Summit doubled as a Bachelor Party for US Delegate Whiteguy. This summit was the rookie year for Brad Wise. In true summit fashion, we took the town by storm. From Cubs game to motorboats, Chicago did not know what hit it.

Summit 2008 - Montreal



The North American Summit 2007 was based in Montreal, Quebec. This Summit was headlined by the Grey Cup. This summit was the rookie year for Paul Witkiewitz. The Spirit of Edmonton, Grey Cup Village and many sports bars along the way held their own against the men of the summit. This summit claimed many livers....

Summit 2009 - Lousiville



The North American Summit 2009 was based in Louisville, Kentucky. This Summit was headlined by the Kentucky Derby. The round table was held in downtown Kentucky. The likes of Paris Hilton and Chuck Ladell joined the summit after the round table to take in an O.A.R. Concert.

Summit 2010 - Shuswap Lake


Summit 2010 - Shuswap Lake The North American Summit 2010 was held on Shuswap Lake, British Columbia. A 3,900 square foot houseboat was the floating palace for the summit this year. Fishing, Waterskiing and Sambuca made this year's summit unforgettable.
